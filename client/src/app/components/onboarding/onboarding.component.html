Provide Pictures

You can choose to either take passport photos or upload pictures from your gallery.

Add to Cart

You can select the sizes of the photos and number of units to print. Then add to the cart and checkout. Or you can clear all items from your cart.

Provide Address

You can provide a delivery address or change your previous one. You need to select the city, zone, and corresponding area.

Confirm Payment

You can view the payment details for your order. You can either pay online or after delivery. For online payment, you wil be redirected to SSL Commerz page.

Get Notified

You can choose to get notified in your phone as well as via email or opt out of notifications.
