Lighting and Background

Position the lamps directly in front of you to eliminate shadows on your face. Find a pale, even-toned wall without any objects for optimal results.

Proper Outfit

Opt for professional attire such as office-style shirts, sweaters, or dresses. Choose garments with higher necklines to ensure they are visible in the final cropped photo.

Posing

Pose naturally, reflecting your everyday appearance. Maintain a neutral expression or natural smile, and keep your eyes open. Face the camera directly with your full face visible.
